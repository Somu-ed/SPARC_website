DGPS Survey and Mapping

SPARC specializes in Differential GPS survey and mapping technique, which provides improved location accuracy than Global Positioning System (GPS). We have successfully completed more than 200+ DGPS survey assignments and pioneers in geo-referencing of forest land and mining areas through DGPS.

Remote Sensing Applications

SPARC has its own expertise in interpretation of satellite imageries since the year 2000 and is also been recognized by National Remote Sensing Centre (Indian Space Research Organization, Department of Space). Our organization has worked extensively in interpretation and processing of satellite images and aerial photographs for diverse types of mapping applications.

Geodatabase Development

SPARC’s GIS designers keep a set of applications in mind as they move ahead with the geodatabase preparation. Defining the end-use helps us to determine the content specification for each theme and how the themes should be represented geographically. The geodatabase design also considers existing spatial data (if any) including their scale and representation.

UAV Survey & Photogrammetry

SPARC adopts Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) based services and Structure From Motion Photogrammetry techniques to generate ultra high resolution (2-5 cm) ortho-images, Digital Elevation Models (DEMs) and contours which are extremely useful for very-large scale mapping (1:100 – 1:1000) and micro-planning.
