GIS Application Development

SPARC’s application development can be broadly classified into 3 ways: Web, Mobile and Cloud Application. The organization has well-established open source development network which facilitates better quality of complete Software Development Life Cycle such as system architecture, development of geodatabase, development of Web GIS application, information portal, testing, deployment etc. SPARC has reliable professionals who develop solutions using this framework which is comparatively cost effective and also allows to focus on quick software updates, bug fixing and better transparency.

GIS Mapping and Analysis

Over the years, SPARC has pioneered deployment of GIS technology (digitisation, DGPS surveys, GIS analysis, photogrammetry, remote sensing assessments etc.) in different vertical markets. Our team has utilized GIS in integrating spatial information on land-use, land cover, slope, elevation, rivers and water bodies etc.

Data Mining

Focusing on the information requirement, data mining involves development and implementation of algorithms for data management and information extraction, mining-out the hidden patterns, identification of unknown co-relations and task specific information.

IoT Applications

SPARC has a standard network infrastructure for development of GIS integrated IoT based solutions. The IoT based solution captures the location coordinate information along with sensor data and reflects the same on a geospatial map platform in real-time mode.

Location-based Information System

SPARC has well legitimate GIS infrastructure setup and time-tested procedural framework for development of Location-based Service solution on all contemporary software platforms. Our organization specializes in development and implementation of GIS integrated Location-based Services solution and utilize its potential in effective decision making.

Business Intelligence

SPARC has an established Business Intelligence framework which facilitates spatial information for management support and decision support in governance as well as in business. The geospatial business intelligence solutions provide features for data mining and extraction of information and helps in enhancing business visibility.
